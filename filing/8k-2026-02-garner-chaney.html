Form 8-K — Resignations of Chairman/Chief Executive Officer and Director

Filed: February 4, 2026 Form: 8-K · Current Report · Item 5.02 Filer: Charlton Aria Acquisition Corporation CIK: 0002024459
Open original on SEC EDGAR Back to filings
Summary. On February 2, 2026, Mr. Robert W. Garner resigned from his positions as Chairman of the Board, Chief Executive Officer, and director of the company, effective immediately. On February 4, 2026, Mr. Mark Chaney resigned from his position as a member of the board of directors, effective immediately. The company has disclosed that neither resignation was the result of any disagreement with the company on any matter relating to the company's operations, policies, or practices.
