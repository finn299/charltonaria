Leadership

A board with institutional reach.

Charlton Aria is led by Chief Executive Officer Jung Min Lee, who also serves as a director and as acting Chief Financial Officer pending the appointment of a permanent CFO. He is supported by a board of independent directors with deep experience across investment management, cross-border transactions, capital markets, and operating leadership.

Management

Chief Executive Officer.

Chief Executive Officer · Director · Acting Chief Financial Officer

Jung Min Lee

Chief Executive Officer, Charlton Aria Acquisition Corporation

Mr. Lee was appointed Chief Executive Officer of Charlton Aria Acquisition Corporation, a member of the board of directors, and the company's acting Chief Financial Officer (pending the appointment of a permanent CFO) effective March 26, 2026. He draws on more than two decades of professional experience spanning strategic consulting, multinational operations, and global finance, and plays a central role in both the governance and execution of the company's long-term business objectives.

As a member of the company's board, Mr. Lee contributes to high-level strategic decision-making, risk oversight, and corporate governance. He collaborates closely with fellow board members and executive leadership to guide the company through critical phases of growth, capital deployment, and organizational transformation. His cross-functional insight allows him to evaluate complex challenges from operational, financial, and human-capital perspectives — bringing clarity and structure to the boardroom.

In his executive leadership role, Mr. Lee oversees the company's internal operations, including finance, accounting, compliance, and administrative infrastructure. His focus on operational discipline and strategic alignment is pivotal to enhancing organizational scalability and investor confidence as the company executes on its acquisition strategy in pursuit of an extraordinary business combination.

Prior to joining Charlton Aria, Mr. Lee served as Vice President at MI Links, a global research and marketing consulting firm, where over a 15-year tenure he led the firm's international consulting practice — helping clients navigate market entry, brand strategy, and customer analytics across Asia, North America, and Europe. Earlier in his career, he served as a Financial Analyst at Merrill Lynch & Company, managing investment portfolios and providing financial market analysis to institutional clients, and began his career at LG Electronics in the Network Business Division's corporate planning office.

Mr. Lee holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Johns Hopkins University Carey Business School and a Bachelor's degree in Western Languages from Hankuk University of Foreign Studies. Fluent in English and Korean, he consistently demonstrates an ability to operate effectively in cross-cultural and multidisciplinary environments.

A strategic thinker, pragmatic operator, and trusted advisor, Mr. Lee continues to shape Charlton Aria's growth trajectory — bridging vision and execution through experienced, ethical, and globally attuned leadership.

Board of directors

Independent directors.

Sponsor representation

From the sponsor.

ST Sponsor II Limited is the company's sponsor and the holder of the founder shares and private placement units. The sponsor is represented in the company's organization by its directors, who are responsible for the day-to-day work of identifying and evaluating prospective business combination targets.

Director of the Sponsor

Sunny Tan Kah Wei

Director, ST Sponsor II Limited

Mr. Tan serves as a director of ST Sponsor II Limited, the company's sponsor. In his role with the sponsor, he is involved in the sourcing, evaluation, and structuring of prospective business combination opportunities for Charlton Aria, and has signed engagement letters and operating agreements on behalf of the sponsor since the company's formation.

Mr. Tan brings cross-border operating and capital markets experience between Asia and the United States to the work of the sponsor.

Recent leadership changes

New chief executive officer appointed; permanent CFO search ongoing.

The company has disclosed a series of leadership changes through current reports on Form 8-K filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. On February 2, 2026, Robert W. Garner resigned from his positions as chairman of the board, chief executive officer, and director, and on February 4, 2026, Mark Chaney resigned from the board of directors. On March 24, 2026, Yuanmei Ma resigned as Chief Financial Officer and director. The company has reported that none of the resignations was the result of any disagreement with the company on any matter relating to the company's operations, policies, or practices.

On March 26, 2026, the board of directors appointed Mr. Jung Min Lee as Chief Executive Officer and a member of the board, and as acting Chief Financial Officer of the company pending the appointment of a permanent CFO. The board continues its process to identify candidates for the permanent CFO role and any other open positions. Material developments will be disclosed through the company's filings with the SEC.
